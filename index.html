📍 Performance Marketing
Scaling Real Estate Leads with Meta Ads
Industry: Real Estate | Client: Dubai & Udaipur-based Properties

The Challenge

The client was running ads with a high cost-per-lead and low-quality enquiries. Budgets were being spent on broad audiences with minimal targeting precision. The creative assets were generic and not tailored to the buyer's journey stage.

The Strategy

  • Built a 3-stage Meta funnel (Awareness → Consideration → Conversion)
  • Designed audience segments by income level, geography, and intent signals
  • A/B tested 4 creative variants per ad set — video walkthroughs vs static visuals
  • Wrote high-urgency ad copy with location-specific hooks
  • Retargeted video viewers with lead form ads

The Execution

  • Deployed campaigns across Meta Ads Manager with daily spend monitoring
  • Killed underperforming ad sets within 72 hours using CPL benchmarks
  • Refreshed creatives bi-weekly to combat ad fatigue
  • Optimised landing page experience for mobile-first traffic
  • Weekly performance reports delivered to client with actionable insights
320+ Leads in 30 days
42% CPL Reduction
3.1% CTR Achieved
📈 Social Media Growth
Growing Instagram from Zero to Engaged Community
Industry: Travel / Lifestyle | Client: Zenith Travel Group

The Challenge

A new account with no audience, no content calendar, and no brand voice. The client needed to build credibility and an engaged following from scratch — without relying purely on paid ads.

The Strategy

  • Developed a brand voice framework — aspirational, warm, and adventure-focused
  • Built a Reels-first content calendar leveraging trending audio + travel hooks
  • Created a signature carousel format — "5 Reasons to Visit [Destination]"
  • Used ChatGPT to generate caption variations tested for hook quality
  • Engaged strategically with niche travel communities

The Execution

  • Posted 5–6 times per week across feed + stories
  • Used analytics to identify peak engagement windows
  • Collaborated on UGC-style Reels to build social proof
  • Ran interactive Stories (polls, countdowns, Q&As) to drive saves and shares
  • Monthly content review with client — pivoting based on top-performing formats
200% Follower Growth (3 months)
4.8% Avg Engagement Rate
12× Reel Reach Increase
🎨 Creative + Branding
Building a Recognisable Brand Through Carousel Design
Industry: Education / Digital Marketing | Client: Digital Rubix

The Challenge

The brand had no visual consistency. Posts looked different every week, the colour palette wasn't fixed, and the audience had no reason to stop scrolling. Engagement was low despite a decent follower count.

The Strategy

  • Created a 3-template carousel system: Educational, Promotional, Social Proof
  • Fixed a brand colour palette and typography — applied across all posts
  • Used the "Hook Slide" method — first slide acts as a scroll-stopper headline
  • Designed ad creatives that matched organic content style for lower ad friction
  • Targeted working professionals aged 24–35 interested in digital skills

The Execution

  • Delivered 8–10 Canva-designed carousels per month
  • Built an ad creative library for Meta retargeting campaigns
  • Tracked saves-per-post as a leading metric for content quality
  • Refreshed visual templates quarterly to stay current with platform trends
  • Coordinated content with campaign timing for product launches
5× Post Save Rate Increase
35% Engagement Rate Lift
28% CTR Improvement on Ads

05 / AI-Powered Workflow

Smarter strategy,
not just harder work

AI tools don't replace strategy — they amplify it. Here's how I integrate AI into every phase of campaign work.

01

Research & Ideation with ChatGPT

I use ChatGPT to generate 10–15 content angle ideas per campaign, identify trending hooks, and research competitor messaging — then filter through strategy, not automation.

02

Caption & Copy Drafting

AI produces first-draft variations of captions and ad copy. I refine tone, inject brand voice, and A/B test the strongest 2 versions. Speed without losing quality.

03

Content Calendar Planning

Monthly content calendars are built using AI to map content pillars against brand goals and seasonal trends — then manually edited for cultural relevance and real-time opportunities.

04

Performance Analysis & Iteration

I feed campaign data back into AI prompts to identify patterns in high-performing creatives and generate hypotheses for the next test round. Data → insight → action.
